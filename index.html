Call Centre Dashboard

Client: Government-affiliated Sustainable Energy Installation Organization

Tools Used: Power BI, Excel, SQL, DAX

Timeline: May 2022 – August 2022

Project Overview:
This dashboard supported a call centre team handling inquiries related to government-approved sustainable energy upgrades. The team guided customers in identifying eligibility and applying for subsidized programs, while managing appointments and installation logistics.

Key Objectives:

  • Track daily and weekly inbound vs. outbound call volumes
  • Monitor agent productivity and unanswered calls
  • Measure conversion rates and RTB (Ready to Book) performance
  • Visualize program-specific engagement and regional RTBs
  • Manage and prioritize pipeline follow-up activity

Dashboard Highlights:

  1. Inbound vs Outbound Overview: Tracks daily/weekly call volumes, % change, agent-wise totals, and unanswered calls.
  2. Conversion Monitoring: Weekly conversion rates for GH and SBP programs with trend insights.
  3. RTB Tracking: Compares weekly RTBs to the 450/week target, with breakdown by region and program type.
  4. Program-Based Call Tracking: Monthly and weekly analysis of GH program calls—split by inbound, outbound, and total.
  5. Pipeline Management: Application statuses across GH, SBP, GU, and HAP; highlights bottlenecks and follow-up backlogs.
  6. Greener Homes CS Pipeline: Tracks attempts per application, average days since last attempt, and status by attempt stage.

Impact:
This dashboard enabled data-driven decision-making for call centre leadership by identifying call trends, improving agent scheduling, increasing conversion efficiency, and prioritizing pipeline follow-ups. It helped meet operational targets like RTB volume and reduced delays in customer engagement.

Skills Used: Power BI, DAX, Data Modeling, Visualizations, SQL, Workflow Optimization
